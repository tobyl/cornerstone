Providing adults & children with fast access to Counselling and Psychological Services in a warm, supportive, and confidential environment.

Cornerstone Family Care was built to eliminate the difficulties people face in accessing supportive services in the Windsor-Essex County Region. Our clinical model, which includes a broad range of counselling and support services, addresses three major needs:

  • Prompt telephone consultation
  • Fast access to counselling appointments with short or no wait times
  • A large group of experienced practitioners qualified to support almost any mental health need

Cornerstone combines the elements of a comfortable atmosphere, efficient administrative services, and an experienced team of practitioners who are readily available to assist you and/or your family through the challenging issues you may be experiencing.

We welcome your telephone inquiries and future visits. Call us today to learn how we can help.
